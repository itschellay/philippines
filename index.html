Discover the Philippines!

The Philippines is a country in Southeast Asia made up of more than 7,641 islands. It has many beautiful places like white sand beaches and old mountains where people grow rice. The country’s history has given it a mix of local ways and things from Spain and America, making it the only country in Asia that is mostly Christian. The people are known around the world for being very friendly, welcoming, and strong. They put their family first and often find a way to stay happy and cheerful, even when things are hard. The Philippines is a fun place with a rich past and lovely, warm people.
